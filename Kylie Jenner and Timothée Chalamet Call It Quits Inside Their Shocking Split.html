Los Angeles, March 18, 2025 – Hollywood’s most unexpected yet adored couple, Kylie Jenner and Timothée Chalamet, have officially ended their whirlwind romance. A Love Story That Took Hollywood by Surprise

When Kylie Jenner, the billionaire beauty mogul, and Timothée Chalamet, the Oscar-nominated actor, first went public with their relationship, fans were stunned. Their romance, which began in early 2023, was full of red carpet appearances, secret getaways, and cozy public outings that left the internet obsessed. But despite their chemistry, cracks in their relationship reportedly began to show in late 2024.

Why Did They Break Up?

According to insiders, Kylie, 27, and Timothée, 29, struggled with their busy schedules and vastly different worlds. Kylie, who is deeply involved in her multi-million-dollar empire and raising her two children, reportedly found it difficult to align her lifestyle with Timothée’s, who is frequently filming and traveling for his movie career.

“There was no drama, no big fight,” an anonymous source revealed. “They just live very different lives, and at some point, it became clear that it wasn’t working.”

Signs of Trouble?

Fans began noticing signs of trouble when Kylie was absent from Timothée’s recent red carpet events, and the actor was missing from the Kardashian-Jenner family’s latest gatherings. Social media detectives also pointed out that the two stopped liking or engaging with each other’s posts online, fueling rumors of a split.

Moving Forward

Sources say Kylie is focusing on her children and business, while Timothée is immersing himself in his upcoming projects, including the much-anticipated Dune: Part Three. Neither Kylie nor Timothée has publicly addressed the breakup yet, but fans are already speculating about their next romantic moves.
