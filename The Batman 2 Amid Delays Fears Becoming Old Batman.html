Robert Pattinson Eager to Start Filming ‘The Batman 2’ Amid Delays

Fans of Robert Pattinson’s Dark Knight portrayal in The Batman have been eagerly awaiting the sequel, but production delays have left even the star himself frustrated. Pattinson recently voiced his impatience, humorously remarking that by the time filming starts, he might be playing "Old Batman."

Initially slated for release in 2025, The Batman 2 has faced multiple setbacks, pushing its expected debut to 2027. The primary reason behind the delay is the script, which director Matt Reeves insists must be perfect before production begins. Reeves, who helmed the critically acclaimed first film, is known for his meticulous storytelling and wants to ensure the sequel maintains the same depth and complexity that made The Batman a hit.

Warner Bros. and DC’s Commitment

Warner Bros. and DC Studios remain committed to the project, despite recent restructuring within DC’s cinematic universe under James Gunn and Peter Safran. Unlike other DC films, The Batman exists in its own standalone universe, free from the constraints of the wider DCU, which allows Reeves to craft his unique take on Gotham without interference.

Robert Pattinson’s Preparation

Pattinson has been actively preparing for the role, maintaining his physique and staying engaged with the vision of Reeves’ Gotham. However, with an uncertain start date, fans and industry insiders speculate whether the long delay might impact the film’s momentum.

Despite the delays, Pattinson is excited to don the cape and cowl once again. His portrayal of Bruce Wayne was widely praised for its dark, brooding depth, and The Batman was a commercial and critical success. Fans are hopeful that the sequel will build upon the first film’s success, introducing more complex villains and expanding the noir-inspired world of Gotham.

Quality Over Speed

While the wait for The Batman 2 may be frustrating, it reassures audiences that the filmmakers are prioritizing quality over speed. Reeves’ careful approach suggests that when the film finally arrives, it will be well worth the anticipation. For now, fans and Pattinson alike will have to be patient, but if the first film is any indication, The Batman 2 is set to be an unforgettable chapter in the Caped Crusader’s story.
