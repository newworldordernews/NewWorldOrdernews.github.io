Elimination Chamber Qualifiers Shake Up Monday Night Raw

The February 17, 2025, episode of WWE Monday Night Raw brought thrilling action as key Elimination Chamber qualifying matches unfolded, paving the way for major storylines heading into the premium live event.

Seth Rollins vs. Finn Balor

Seth Rollins, "The Visionary," secured his spot in the highly anticipated Elimination Chamber match by overcoming a fierce challenge from Finn Balor. The intense back-and-forth match had the crowd on the edge of their seats, with Rollins sealing the win with his signature stomp.

Roxanne Perez Qualifies

On the women’s side, Roxanne Perez emerged victorious against Raquel Rodriguez, thanks to a distraction caused by the formidable duo of Bianca Belair and Naomi. This shocking interference added a layer of intrigue, hinting at potential alliances or rivalries that could shake up the women’s division.

Dakota Kai Number One Contender

Meanwhile, Dakota Kai triumphed over Ivy Nile to become the number one contender for the Women's Intercontinental Championship. Kai’s victory showcased her resilience and tactical prowess, setting the stage for a clash with the current champion that fans are eagerly anticipating.

Conclusion

The night was filled with electrifying moments and dramatic turns, with these results adding fuel to the build-up toward the Elimination Chamber event. As the chamber lineup takes shape, WWE fans are bracing themselves for an unforgettable showdown inside the unforgiving steel structure.

Stay tuned as more qualifiers are expected to take place in the coming weeks!
