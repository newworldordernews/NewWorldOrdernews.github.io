LISA's Solo Album 'Alter Ego' Receives Mixed Reviews from Fans and Critics

February 26, 2025 – BLACKPINK’s LISA, one of the most recognized K-pop idols worldwide, has released her highly anticipated debut solo album, Alter Ego. The album, featuring 13 tracks, aims to showcase different sides of LISA’s personality, but reactions from fans and critics have been mixed.

BLACKPINK's LISA releases her solo album 'Alter Ego,' receiving mixed reviews from fans and critics.

Exploring LISA's Alter Egos

The album presents LISA’s alter egos—Vixi, Roxi, and Sunni—each representing different aspects of her musical style and persona. With collaborations from global artists like Future, Megan Thee Stallion, and Ty Dolla $ign, Alter Ego was expected to make a major impact on the global pop and hip-hop scene.

Mixed Reviews from Fans and Critics

While some fans appreciate the album’s high-energy beats and LISA’s charismatic presence, others feel that it lacks originality and depth. One of the main criticisms is that Alter Ego relies heavily on trends rather than pushing musical boundaries. Several tracks have been described as formulaic, following a pattern already familiar in Western pop and hip-hop.

Chart Success and Social Media Reactions

Despite the criticism, Alter Ego has performed well on global charts, debuting in the top 10 of major streaming platforms such as Spotify and Apple Music. The lead single, Phantom, received millions of streams within hours of release, proving LISA’s massive influence in the music industry.

The Future of LISA’s Solo Career

Fans remain divided over the album’s direction. Some celebrate LISA’s solo venture as a strong statement of her identity outside BLACKPINK, while others hoped for a more experimental sound. Whether she will take the feedback into account for her next release remains to be seen, but one thing is certain—LISA’s global star power continues to shine.
