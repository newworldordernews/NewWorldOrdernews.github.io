J-Hope Announces 'Hope On The Stage World Tour' for 2025: Dates, Ticket Sales, and More

J-Hope, a member of the globally popular K-pop group BTS, has revealed his solo 'Hope On The Stage World Tour,' set to kick off in March 2025. The U.S. leg will begin in Brooklyn, New York, on March 13 and 14, with ten shows planned across North America. After completing his mandatory military service in October 2024, J-Hope is preparing for his return with new music. His latest EP, "HOPE ON THE STREET VOL. 1," was released in 2024, showcasing his unique blend of pop and hip-hop. Fans can purchase tickets starting January 17, 2025, via platforms like Vivid Seats, with a 100% buyer guarantee. This tour follows J-Hope’s successful solo performance at Lollapalooza in 2022 and is expected to draw massive excitement from fans worldwide, as he continues to expand his musical career.
