BTS Announces Special Episodes of "Run BTS!" to Celebrate Its Phenomenal Success

The globally beloved K-pop sensation BTS has announced a special series of episodes for their hit variety show, Run BTS! to commemorate the show's massive success and their unwavering fan support. First launched in 2015, Run BTS! has become a cultural phenomenon, showcasing the group's camaraderie, humor, and unique personalities through exciting challenges and heartfelt moments. The upcoming episodes promise to deliver never-before-seen concepts, including interactive fan participation, a nostalgic throwback to the shows most iconic moments, and high-energy games that only BTS could make legendary. Fans, known as ARMY, can expect a mix of emotional reflections and laughter as BTS reminisces on their journey so far. These celebratory episodes aim to bridge the gap between the groups ongoing global music domination and their ability to stay intimately connected with fans through candid and relatable content. With the group currently focusing on individual projects, this special series is a heartfelt gift to ARMYs worldwide, reaffirming BTS’s commitment to entertaining and inspiring their audience. Run BTS! special episodes will air on South Korea V LIVE and Weverse platforms, with translations available for international fans, making it a truly global event. Don’t miss this epic celebration of a show that has brought endless joy to millions
