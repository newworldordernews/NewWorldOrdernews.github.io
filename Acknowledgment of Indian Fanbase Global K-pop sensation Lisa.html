Lisa Acknowledges Indian Fanbase, Hints at Future Visit

Global K-pop sensation Lisa, a member of BLACKPINK, has officially acknowledged her ever-growing Indian fanbase for the first time. In a heartfelt message shared on social media, Lisa expressed her gratitude towards her Indian fans for their unwavering love and support. This rare recognition has thrilled her followers in India, who have been eagerly waiting for such an acknowledgment from the superstar.

Lisa’s Message to Indian Blinks

Lisa’s message not only highlighted her appreciation for the dedication of her Indian supporters but also hinted at a potential visit to India in the future. "I am so grateful for all the love and support from my Indian Blinks! Your energy and passion mean so much to me. I hope to see you all soon!" she shared.

Excitement Among Indian K-pop Fans

This announcement has sparked excitement among Indian K-pop fans, with many speculating about possible concerts or fan events in the country. BLACKPINK’s popularity in India has been steadily rising, and Lisa’s message is seen as a significant step toward stronger engagement with her Indian audience. Fans are now eagerly hoping for more interactions and perhaps an official tour date for India in the near future.
